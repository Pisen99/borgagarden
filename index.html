Welcome to Borgagarden

An inn since 1948. Today it's used for our fine-dining restaurant, saloon pub & conferences.

Our Menu Book a Table

Life as an inn owner.

Spring 2018 we took over to drive inn Borgagarden in Borgafjäll. None of us had the experince in this bussines altough we knew this is what we would like to do togheter. After a few "Learning by doing" - scenarios, we're today living the dream a lot of others want to experince. To be creative in your own terms & create the enviroment that we would have loved to visit when going on vacation.


Who are "we" then?

We are Joakim & Cecilia Engwall. Both born -86 & married each other here in Borgafjäll 2014. Today we live in Borgafjäll with our sons Malte & Melker.

Since childhood, Jocke has spent a lot of time here in Borgafjäll as he comes from Dorotea & his parents always had a sports cabin up here. Jocke is a carpenter with a good eye for the beautiful & with his creative side he creates amazing things - you just wait with excitement for what comes next!

Cecilia comes from northern Jämtland and has skied quite a bit in Borgafjäll before she met Jocke who helped her rediscover Borgafjäll. She has worked for many years in the trade sector and her 'servicemindedness' will come in handy at Borgagården. Before the purchase of Borgagården, she retrained and took a commercial helicopter license with dreams of one day flying in her home mountains.

We also have a partner in the company named Andreas Nenzèn who lives and runs his other company Björnberget in Östersund. He likes to come to Borgafjäll and help out and make sure we take care of ourselves.


And so the days go by..

Running a seasonal inn is a way of life. You work all hours of the day, every day, all year round & you are always free. We usually say that Borgagården has two different sides. The winter season offers snow-sure adventures such as skiing and snowmobiling. The summer/autumn season where we bring home animals on the farm & enjoy nice hiking, fishing, mushroom picking, mountain snorkeling, S.U.P etc.

4-bed cabin

These timber cabins with a genuine mountain feel were built for 4 people.

- There are two bunk beds in an open plan layout with a small kitchenette.

- The kitchen has a microwave, coffee maker, fridge & simple household utensils that are needed in a small self-contained household.

- Cleaning items are included as final cleaning is expected of the guest (if you have not purchased for final cleaning, of course).

- Toilet with sink & shower.

- We have both animal-free cabins and cabins where animals are welcome. So be sure to let us know if you are bringing the extra family member with you when you book. Animals cost NOK 100 extra per cabin/stay.

6-bed cabin

These cottages have a nice location and have a small balcony that faces Lake Borgasjön and Borgahällan.

- There are two bedrooms with a bunk bed in each bedroom & two sofa beds in the living room.

- The kitchen has an oven, microwave, coffee maker, fridge & simple household utensils that are needed in a self-contained household.

- Cleaning items are included as final cleaning is expected of the guest (if you have not purchased for final cleaning, of course).

- TV is available with the basic range of channels.

- Toilet with shower.

- We have both animal-free cabins and cabins where animals are welcome. So be sure to let us know if you are bringing the extra family member with you when you book. Costs NOK 100 extra per cabin/stay.

Tallbacken

We offer a cabin close to the adventure!

- In the cabin there is a dishwasher (because there are more fun things to do in the mountains), gas / induction hob & oven, wood-burning stove.

- Fiber with wireless internet (In cases where there are disturbances in the connection, we are not liable for compensation as this is a free service).

- Underfloor heating in bathroom and hall, electric sauna.

- Pets are welcome.

- Address: Borgaliden 103.

The Cowboy Hut

We offer a newly built cabin that is out of the ordinary. Living in the cowboy cabin is an experience in itself where there is a thought behind every unique detail

- In the cabin are dishwasher, Gas / induction hob & oven, wood burning stove.

- Fiber with wireless internet (In the event that there are disturbances in the connection, we are not liable for compensation as this is a free service)

- Underfloor heating in bathroom,

- Possibility of sauna, according to agreement on site.

- Pets are welcome.

- 6 + 2 beds.

Tent & Caravan

Borgagården also offer some nice but few places for tents & caravans/motorhomes.

- With us, you can set up your tent in the most beautiful place in Borgafjäll, in our opinion.

- Namely on our headland down by the lake.

- You park your car up by the main building, where you also have access to a shower and toilet in our electric sauna.

- If you have a caravan or mobile home with you, you can park next to our 4-bed cottages where you have access to electricity.

- Shower and toilet in our electric sauna are close by.
